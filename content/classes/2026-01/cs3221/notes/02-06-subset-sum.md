Today's problem:

- We've got an integer `x` and a list of integers `ys`.
- Can we pick some of the numbers from `ys` such that they
  add up exactly to `x`?
- Answer: null or the list

Example:

- 100, (10, 20, 27, 30, 50, 18) -> (20, 30, 50)
- 100, (23, 34, 45, 76) -> null
