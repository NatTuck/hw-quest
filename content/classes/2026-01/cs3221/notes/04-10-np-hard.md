We've looked at a bunch of different complexity classes: O(log n), O(n), O(n log
n), O(n^2), O(n^30), O(2^n), etc.

A broader way to break things down is into just two categories:

- Polynomial time: O(n^k) for some constant k.
- Slow (probably exponential time)
